# Chapter 1: Physical AI Fundamentals - Specification

## Chapter Overview

This chapter introduces students to the fundamental concepts of Physical AI, distinguishing it from traditional AI approaches and establishing the foundation for understanding how AI systems interact with physical environments. The chapter builds from basic definitions to more complex interactions between AI and physical systems.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, students will be able to:

1. Define Physical AI and distinguish it from traditional AI approaches
2. Explain how AI systems interact with physical environments
3. Identify the core components of Physical AI systems (perception, reasoning, action)
4. Analyze examples of Physical AI applications
5. Understand the challenges in creating AI-physical system interactions

## Target Audience

- Students with basic programming knowledge
- Learners with basic math/physics background
- No prior robotics experience required
- Appropriate for beginner to intermediate level
